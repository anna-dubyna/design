What is minimalism

Minimalism isn't just about throwing everything away and leaving white walls.
It is a mindset that allows you to take everything you need for a happy life,
but not more.

Not at the cost of air pollution, not at the cost of landfills, not at the cost of
lives of animals and fish, which die because a person wants more and more.
